About Us

  • Cross of Mercy Ministries is bringing the love of Jesus to a hurting world specifically Uganda. Started in Canada in mid 2000. God raised up a Ugandan Minister, Sarah Kanamwangi, and sent her to her country as a Missionary/Evangelist.
  • In addition to evangelizing through open air meetings and Church planting God began to show other needs of the young children in the communities. Cross of Mercy Ministries provides education, literature and compassion ministy including necessities, caregiving and feeding to these ones.
  • Cross of Mercy Ministries partners with other Mission organization in Uganda which brings a greater impact to the local people.
  • Prayer is a vital part of the Ministry.
